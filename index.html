The Vegan Mexican

Fortunately, many foods from Mexican culture, such as rice, beans, and fruits, are naturally vegan, and many traditional dishes do not call for animal-based ingredients. The Vegan Mexican is a patchwork of culture, community, and comida threading plant-based eaters to the Mexican palate.
